QT33 Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-22

QT33 app is used for utility operations data entry, reporting, and synchronized field workflows.

Data We Collect

  • Account profile data (name, email, role, substation mapping)
  • Operational records entered by authorized users
  • Technical sync metadata (device id, timestamps, sync status)

How We Use Data

  • Provide authenticated access and role-based operations
  • Enable web-mobile data sync and audit traceability
  • Generate operational reports and exports

Storage and Sync

The app may store temporary local/offline data on device for continuity. When connectivity is available, pending records are synchronized to backend services.

Sharing

QT33 does not sell personal data. Data is shared only with authorized operational administrators and system services required for app operation.
